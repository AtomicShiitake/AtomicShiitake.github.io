Approaches


Four main types of approaches:

ESA - Energy Separating Agent


Add or remove heat from system
~ Exploits Thermodynamics properties (Tbp and Tmp)
~ Unit ops:
• Distillation
• Crystallization
Benefits:
• High purity
• Simple idea/concept
• High capacity
Drawbacks:
• High cost

MSA - Material Separating Agent

~ Exploits Thermodynamics properties solubility
~ Unit ops:
• Absorption Column
• Stripping Column
• Liquid-Liquid Extraction
Benefits:
• Inexpensive
• High capacity
Drawbacks:
• Not a final step
• Need to identify a sutiable solvent

Solid Agents

~ Exploits Adsorptivity, Van der walls, and dispersion
~ Unit ops:
• Adsorption Column
• Chromatography
• Ion-Exchange
Benefits:
• High purity
Drawbacks
• Limited Capacity
